Projects

GOLF USA

This application was built for a local Minnesota golf retail store. The store had a sales reward program that customers could participate in to earn money off future purchases. The store used paper punch cards to keep track of these rewards. For every $10 a customer spends they receive a punch, after they accumulted 15 punches(fill up a card), they recieve $10 of their next purchase. This was a hastle to maintain and manage especially when some customers would accumulate 10 or more cards in a single purchase.
